We are a collective of graduate students studying media and activism in the digital age.

Dear Altadena is a postcard project that invites local residents to reflect and imagine.

We invite you to share your favorite memories or your vision for the future of Altadena by visiting us at one of our pop-ups or filling out and submitting one of the postcard prompts in the drop-down menu.

The goal of Dear Altadena is to produce a digital compilation of postcards in time for the first anniversary of the fires, which will be available on this site in the coming months!
